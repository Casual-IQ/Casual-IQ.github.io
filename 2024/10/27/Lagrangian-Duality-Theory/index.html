Lagrangian Duality Theory

Background

In an optimization problem, the primal problem often comes with numerous constraints, making it challenging to solve directly. Therefore, one approach is to transform the primal problem into its dual problem and solve the latter to obtain the solution to the original problem. Duality is a core concept in convex optimization.
